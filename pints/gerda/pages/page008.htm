Saw the advert on a university rag where a former classmate of mine used to write. I told him I was on the lookout for creative writing workshops, and he told me he'd keep his eyes open. He kept his promise and sent me a copy of the classified ads from his newspaper. The advert told me to send a manuscript to a certain address in Malate. I sent one, a short story about a homosexual page in Feudal France (don't ask. I was younger), around ten pages, I think.

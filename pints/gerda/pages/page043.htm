I try to say the words, try to ask her, maybe ask who the father is, maybe even the child's name. Anything.

"Let me pay for the beer…" she tells me as she tries to look for her wallet. No, no, I tell her, waving my hand. I… I'll pay for it. She smiles at me, taking a moment to look at me. We stare at each other. Still so pretty, I tell myself. Still seventeen. Should I kiss her? I ask myself. I was considering it when she pulls something out of her wallet and gives it to me. I take it.

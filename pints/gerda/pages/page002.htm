Anyway, one Monday night
I was in the Ortigas station, waiting for the north bound train, about fifteen minutes before the MRT stations closed. I spent most of the day looking for a book that I didn't even find. I was pretty frustrated (I bet you know how that feels like), decided to go home. The second to the last train was coming when I suddenly hear someone call my name from across the rails. It was a familiar voice, knew it by heart, actually.

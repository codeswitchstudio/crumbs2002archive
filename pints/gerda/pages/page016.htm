My name is Sid, I told her. "Uh-huh," she muttered without even looking. "For Sidney?" she asked me. No, no, I said, shaking my head. Sid for Siddharta.

She stopped scribbling and looked at me, straight, our eyes locking. She had the most beautiful eyes, they look like they have lights behind them. The sun, maybe? We were silent for about half a minute, we could hear the sea crashing on the rocks seventy feet below us.

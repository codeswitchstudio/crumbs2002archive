I remember she used to have this American accent, really straight and clear, and she used to talk fast, like a bloody machine gun. Now, her Rs are rolling a bit, her As are deeper, more round… and she takes her time with the syllables. Her accent matured. And I just couldn't quite pin it down on just one accent: a bit of French, a bit of Spanish, a bit of English and Tagalog thrown in. A hybrid of accents.

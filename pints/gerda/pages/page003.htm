I was reading a book on leather binding, seated on a bench, a bit behind one of
the pillars. When I heard my name, I was up and about, looking left and right. And from across the rails, I saw someone waving at me. I know her, I remember thinking.

Gerda? I call back. "Siddharta! It IS you!" she shouts back at me. The distance was about twenty feet, and the on- coming train was blaring its klaxons, so we had to shout.

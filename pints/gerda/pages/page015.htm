I sat beside her, crossing my legs. I brought my notepad with me, and I put it facedown in front of me. She glanced towards me, wasn't even more than five seconds, then she returned to her writing.

Hi, I said, trying to strike up a conversation. She gave me another glance, this time a bit longer, and a smile. "Hello," she said rather unexcitedly before returning to her notepad. I noticed her dimples as she smiled.

I tried again.

"Yeah, Greece. About a week from now." she said. I felt my legs turn into jelly. Shit, I remember thinking. How long will you be in Athens? I asked her, hope draining from my voice with every syllable uttered.

"I'm going there to study. Four, maybe five years. Not too sure." The words were like dark clouds. I remember shivering then. Imagine that: shivering in summer.

Fancy meeting you here, I tell Gerda as I walk towards her. She smiles and hugs me. I hug her back. Where are you heading? I ask her.

"Home. Came from the office, actually," she tells me as we break our embrace. She puts down her bag and stretches her arms. "Man, this bag is heavy." She turns to me. "How have you been? It's been, what? Five years?"

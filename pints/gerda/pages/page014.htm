One afternoon, I decided to spend my day under the mango tree, maybe smoke some grass and write. The day started out pretty dull, so I thought maybe smoking would make it better.

I saw Gerda scribbling on her notepad, cross-legged underneath the mango tree's shade. She wore this hippie skirt with Indian gods printed all over it, and it also had those small circular mirrors woven into the cloth itself. I remember my mother wearing one of those things.

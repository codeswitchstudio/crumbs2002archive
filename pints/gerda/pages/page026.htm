"Oh, you know," she tells me with a shrug of her shoulders. "Went to Athens to study, learned some new things, drifted around Europe for a while, doing odd jobs here and there: a baker's apprentice in Italy, a shopkeeper in Belgium, a librarian in London… met the people, saw the sights, heard the sounds…" she tells me as she counts them off with her fingers.

That's when I noticed her accent. It changed.

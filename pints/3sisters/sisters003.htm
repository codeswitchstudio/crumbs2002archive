So there I was. THREE SISTERS, the night after, 10 in the PM. Waiting for Saffron. I ordered a pint already, with around half of it in my stomach. My pen and notebook combo is on top of the table, side by side, within reach. Just in case.

THREE SISTERS music is pretty much diverse and different from the other places in the neighborhood. There's rock, but it's JON SPENCER BLUES EXPLOSION. There's the light sentimental sort, but it's BELLE & SEBASTIAN. There are also the occasional spoken-word poetry tapes, and the occasional ENYA records. Every night it's different. Flavor of the Night, is what the barkeep calls it.

"Hi," Saffron-through-the-answerphone said. "I'm in the toilet right now, shitting bricks, so I'm pretty much out of the 'phonebox's reach. Message after the beep, and I'll get back to you when I get the time. Ciao!"

*beep*

"Saffron, it's Sid," I say to Saffron-through-the-answerphone. "THREE SISTERS, tomorrow at 10 in the PM. Usual table, my turn to spend. Bring a pen and a notebook. Or maybe I'll bring mine. Whatever. Okay, that's all. THREE SISTERS, tomorrow at 10. See you when I see you."

And I place the receiver back on the 'phonebox.

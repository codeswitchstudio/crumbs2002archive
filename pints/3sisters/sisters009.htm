"How have you been? Any new girls in your life?" she asks.

I drink before answering, noticing that she hasn't touched her beer, yet. I point at it while drinking. She notices, and then drinks from her pint.

I've been okay, I guess, I tell her. A new job, a new place, new projects. No new girlfriends, though I'm looking for one. Call me if you know someone who'd like to hook up with a writer-type of guy, hairy in the right places and well-proportioned, I tell her jokingly.

 

Robin herself was only 26, an undergrad. She dropped out of MassComm when we were together. In the background, ASTROBOY 2010 is doing their version of B&S' 'Is it wicked not to care?'. I have to get a copy of this album, I thought. I nodded, saying yes, it's that time of the year again, and that, yes, my birthday's on next week, and that there's a party of sorts, and told her she's invited. She smiles, and my heart smiles with her. Back when the two of us were together, making her smile was in my everyday To Do list. She had one of those smiles, you know? She smiles, and the world smiles with her.

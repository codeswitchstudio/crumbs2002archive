SAFFRON? I call to her as soon as I was inside her place. On the way, I bought three six-packs of beer. "In here, my room." she calls out to me. I walk to her kitchen and shove the beer inside the fridge.

"What did you buy?" she asks me as I enter her room. Despite the situation, I smile at the way her room smells. It hits me in a way that warm wind hits you when you come out of a cold theater, or if you come out of an air-conditioned office. The room smells feminine. Soft powder and faint

incense and… I don't know what else. Heady smells.

Saffron is sitting up in bed, over her blankets, her head and back against the wall. She's still in her Winnie the Pooh pajamas. I sit at the foot of the bed.

I bought beer. They're in the kitchen. She nods, and I notice that her eyes are still watery. Red and watery. "In bottles?" I shake my head rather disappointedly. In can. She sticks her tongue out. I smile. Had no choice, as they were all in cans. She smiles. "They'll do, don't worry." And with that she stands up and walks towards the door. Reluctantly, I follow her.

I still can't wrap my brain on it, Saffron. I mean, Elvin and Rhoda. Dead, just like that. I tell her while we drink around the kitchen counter.

There were around five of us in the classroom when that happened: Gerard and I huddled in one corner, an acoustic gui-
-tar cradled over my thigh, Allan by the door, Chinky-Eyed Boy by his seat, and this girl classmate of ours named Michelle (first tier, sixth from left), who was reading Dekada '70.
The sound was so shocking and unexpected, the two of us jumped two inches from our seats. Strange thing was, that guy always was a bit of a dandy. We thought that show of machismo was funny, the two of us were giggling for about ten minutes or so after the incident.
Distracted by the past, I decided to nip on down to the cellar to get a bottle of Magic. Decided to take the photograph with me, sentimental bastard that I am. Well, I had an excuse. It's not everyday that memories from my high school years come trickling back to the front of my brain, you know.

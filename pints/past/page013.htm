Went home five hours later, drunk as a skunk, and just as smelly. When I was sober enough, I put my clothes into the washing machine. Remembered about the photograph inside my left butt pocket, panicked, opened the machine door while it was spinning.
The photograph was ruined, all the color eaten away by the bleach in the detergent.
Lost, but not forgotten.

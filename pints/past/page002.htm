Anyway, was looking through my files, looking for the sci-fi short, when I stumbled upon a piece of my past. Way, way older than the short. Actually, it's even older than my writing career, whatever that is.

I was in junior high, by the looks of it. A little water-damaged in some places, a missing face here and there. But all in all, the photograph was in good condition. Man, we were so young back then. I was… how old? Fifteen? Sixteen? I was young. Spotty in some places, and I still had most of my hair. I was wearing one of those gray high-cut boots that were in the rave at around that time.

I can still recognize some of the faces.

It was my class picture, circa 1986.

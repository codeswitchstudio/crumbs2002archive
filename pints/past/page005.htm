The short can wait, I thought.
I left the photograph on the living room table (glass top, black metal frame, rectangular, Japanese) and I went down the cellar.
Decided to inspect the photograph a bit further. Jorge, Gerard, Katrina, Chinky-Eyed Boy… who else?
I turn the cap counter-clockwise, opening the bottle. Poured myself a glass of not so fine liquor. Good enough for my purposes, I thought. I had no plans on writing my THE ADVENTURES OF WHITE WOLF AND BLACK BLADE, yet. JD can wait.
Then I remembered how most of us used to write names at the back of the photograph. Maybe I used to do that, too, I thought. After a sip or two, I'll check, I promised myself.

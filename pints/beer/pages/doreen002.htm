I met Doreen Vista back in college.

Svelte and petite, she drove us guys nuts but if you stared hard at her long enough, or at least as much as I had, she wasn't really beautiful. Her eyes were so small and almost assymmetrical, always darting to and fro, scrutinizing the room; perhaps to see if she was being seen. Up close, her skin was so pale you could see delicate pink spider webs beneath her cheeks. Doreen's teeth were so long she actually brandished them during those rare times she smiled in my direction.

You could say I followed her around but not in a deliberate way, like a pathetic stalker would. Doreen just always turned up in my photos as she jumped from one circle of people to another. Here, wait. Lemme get my clearbook and show you.

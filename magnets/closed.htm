Eyes closed, Saffron listened to the 4AM rain do a tap dance on the roof. "Sid takes the most beautiful pictures with his words. He has his own way of seeing things...." she thought, while removing a book from her bed in the dark.

The dark. The dark. Half awake, she remembers the dark.

And the dark twin pools of Sid's sad eyes. She remembers looking for herself in them and letting herself go adrift.....

"God, it's Tuesday!" (Used to be the days were a blur of interchangeable blahs.)

And so Saffron drifted off to a slumber, a bit overwhelmed yet comforted by the thought that each day was becoming more unlike the one before.

-Circe

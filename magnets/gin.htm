It was a Tuesday afternoon when they took our class picture. Our classes start at about seven in the morning. I lived a couple of blocks from school, so I was always early (since I was a Freshman, even). That particular month (March), my classmates and I had been smuggling liquor to school, hidden inside our bags, beneath stacks of textbooks and PE uniforms.

There was this one guy named Laurence who brought liquor inside his. That Tuesday, he brought gin diluted in clear soda, about one part gin mixed with six parts soda. There was too much soda, you could barely taste the gin in it. Some classmates decided to jump the fence and buy more gin from a local store, to give the drink a bit of kick.

Half an hour before our class picture taking, half of the boys in class were already drunk (including me, but not shit-faced, yet. only red). Some were dozing it off on their desks, some were still drinking, some were harassing the girls in the class.

They finally took our picture, with half of the boys missing. I was in the picture, though, red as a strawberry.

And I was smiling. -Juli

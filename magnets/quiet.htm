"You have nice hands," he said.

I was absolutely quiet for about half a minute, staring at my palms.

The lines were light as fresh spider webs. The creases seemed like they were made by a pen that kept on writing even though it had already ran out of ink.

(Did he just say they almost looked like paws? )

An old soul, (Read my boldtype oh Palm-readers!) his lines were deeply etched. More earthy, I suppose. Earthy and sensual versus light and ethereal? In my head lay a vast galaxy of more contrasts.

"God, they're beautiful, your hands." A strange thing to hear and yet
that was the nicest thing anyone noticed about me all week.

There's nothing like holding a hand that feels right. Harmony for about half a minute. If life was a hard rock, perhaps moments like that were its shimmer.

-Circe

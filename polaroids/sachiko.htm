SACHIKO


I took this one on the bizarre Samedi-Dimanche turn-over. It has Sachiko covering her mouth with both hands, eyes wide, like elliptical saucers, her elbows on the tabletop. The bar is darkly-lit, a bit yellow on some corners, like being inside a beer bottle. We drank super subs the whole hour we were there, nursing them up until three in the morning.

I think she's trying to stifle a yawn, here.
                                                                          - Siddharta

ME, IN BED

First one was a picture of me, a bit of a candid shot, sitting up on my bed. Nice effect in the picture was that it looked like a still from a movie. It was grainy and fuzzy in all the right places. The photo got me to thinking: who'd take a picture of me in bed? Obviously, the picture had to be at least five years old, as I looked pretty young in the photo.

It also looked like it was early morning when the picture was taken, which means that I slept with the girl who took the picture (thinking it was a girl who took the photograph, as I've never ever slept with a man before in me entire life. As near as I can remember, at least). - Sid

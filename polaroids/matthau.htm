Walter Matthau

Another one from the bizarre Samedi-Dimanche turn-over. It's a picture of this big old Yank talking on the phone. He's so tall, he has to bend to reach the receiver. He's outside this Korean deli, in his blue jacket and trousers, and he looks a lot like Walter Matthau.

This one comes with a bizarre story.

You see, we went to this Korean deli to buy some beer as it was pretty expensive when you buy them from the bloody pubs. Anyway, we went to the freezers and pulled out a bottle of Red Horse. We've been drinking for about six hours, now, so we thought one bottle of kick-ass beer would be enough for the two of us (that's me and Sachiko, as Trackshine is the designated driver). Also managed to grab a pack of the much-hunted, much-desired Choc*Nuts, and a couple of packs of fish crackers.

Anyway, while we were at the counter to pay for our stuff, this big old Yank walks to my side and puts his bottle of beer on top of the counter, beside our stuff. At the corner of my eyes, I saw that he bought Red Horse, too. I guess he noticed that, as he leaned towards me and with his low Nick Cave-ish voice he said to my ear

"If you want to take a ride, take Red Horse beer!!"

and I looked up at his Walter Matthau mug and said

"Yeah, you got that right, man!!"

and he gives out a hearty belly laugh and we slap palms and he hugs me like I was his long lost kid.

Trackshine, Sachiko and I leave the deli laughing. We just had a close-encounter with a drunken Walter Matthau doppelganger. -Sid

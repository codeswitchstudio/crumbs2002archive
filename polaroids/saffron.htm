Saffron and the Book of Erotic Art
(vols 1 & 2)


this one was taken about a week ago. we were at this art center, hanging out with some friends. they were having this rather extensive workshop. so extensive, most of them had to bring their stuff and spend the night there in
the center.

saffron and i were behind the makeshift bookshelves, where the bed spreads were. only a small part of the room, the darkest corner, even, as the fluorescent lights were at the center of the place, and the bookshelves cast these huge shadows on our corner.

that's me on the left (obviously), all in black. saffron on the right, in one of her sleeveless shirts, the both of us seated on this bean-bag seat full of tiny foam balls (which reminded me of my chemistry projects from way way way back). our feet are stretched out before us. completely relaxed. we are reading this thick thick book called BOOK OF EROTIC ART, VOLS 1 & 2.

pretty much what you'd expect from a book with that sort of title: images from the Kama Sutra (which still haunts me, for some strange reason) to French line drawings (one guy in particular had a great hand with drawing hair) to pop-art
collages. in some of them, you had to squint and turn the book to its side to understand what the drawing was.

it even had prints from an old chinese sex-humor book. it had these great muscular men with penises the size of treetrunks, their members supported by makeshift tripods, made from bamboo. we were both laughing, but for some
strange reason, i felt a bit insecure.

after erotic art, we attacked the children's books section of the bookshelves.

we arrived at the center at about 5PM, and we left at about 12AM. the sky was indigo-dark when we finally left. like india ink with stars for bubbles. -Siddharta

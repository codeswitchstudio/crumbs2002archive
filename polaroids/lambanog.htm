Half-eaten Stuff

contributed by GR_M

It was the moments after the guests left that this ink polaroid was shot. It shows a glass round table filled with used plastic cups, empty pitchers and a half-eaten plate - my half-eaten plate. The alcohol started kicking in just a few minutes ago. It was borne from a plasic cup filled with a brew of lambanog and Sprite. It had to be consumed so as to make sure no drop goes to waste. The plate had an uneaten piece of chicken on it as well as a strip of sparerib steak chopped down to bite-sized proportions. Sure beat the hell out of pork-and-beans every other night!! There were no flies in the background, just the bliss of a drunk after a night of excesses. That and the strange silence that comes after the storm, after the great deluge of alcohol, a deluge worthy of the god of wine himself.

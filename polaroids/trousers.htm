TROUSERS

This one was taken about six years ago, an early Saturday morning in March. Marc and I are standing in front of their garage's main entrance. God, we were so young back then. I had one of those geeky haircuts, parted in the middle, while Marc wore his hair long. Marc's wearing his school uniform as it was Recognition Day, and he was one of the honor students. I'm not wearing mine, as we didn't have any classes anymore, and I wasn't one of the recipients.

Instead I'm wearing this pale blue collared shirt with buttons in front and short sleeves. I sort of look like a Mormon, actually. I had two pairs of trousers on, one inside the other. The one inside was my Physical Ed. jogging trousers. The one outside was this dark-blue pair my aunt made me. Made of cotton, it was. Nice fit and all. The reason I was wearing two pairs was that the dark-blue pair's zipper wasn't working. It was stuck, so I was walking around with the zipper open. So, to avoid ridicule from schoolmates, I wore an extra pair inside. I wasn't even supposed to be there that day. I went because I was asked by Marc's mother to come to the Recognition Day as a photographer.

After the celebrations, I was shooting the bull with Vanessa when she noticed my zipper. "Look!" she told me, her right hand over her mouth, her left pointing at my crotch. "Your zipper's open!" I opened the zipper and showed her that I had an extra pair of trousers inside.

I still have that dark-blue pair of trousers in my closet. - Juli

Head dress

Now, this one's one of the oldest. This is me when I was two years old, I think. My parents made it a point to bring me to every punk concert they did (as my parents started the whole 80s local punk scene. Now you know who to blame). They also made it a point to dress me up like anything. I have this neon-orange Native American head-dress and a leather choker with spikes around it, and wearing my black and grey Batman costume. Imagine that: a baby, his big brown eyes looking straight at you, cheeky bastard, with a feathered head-dress and a leather choker with spikes. My dad is with me in the picture, holding me up. He has his own leather choker, but his only has blunt studs. His hair is a bit orange, though. Right now, the only Native American head-dress I wear isn't orange. It's made of green parrot feathers. -Adam

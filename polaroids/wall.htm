Wall-clutter

This one has Sachiko in it, in one of her sleeve-less shirts. It's a Monday afternoon, sunny outside and all. The type where I used to go out to play on when I was but a wee lad. Sachiko's hair is still short, the sides tucked unto the flaps of her ears.

Sachiko is staring at the PeeCee monitor, right hand on her chin. The PeeCee has a name, and it's Pepe. It was named after the Looney Tunes skunk, the one who used to chase that cat with the white paint strip on her back. The speakers are playing Ben Folds's 'Alice Childress'. a nice song, that was.

The wall behind Pepe is made up of posters and drawings and documents. A wall of color and words, held up by strips of adhesive tape. There's a twelve-page comic strip on the right hand-side, several poems written on colored paper on the left. Water-colored art here and there, care of Leon and Lily. Pages and posters from various magazines are there, too, for variation and whatnot: Beastie Boys, big sky mind, chinese stationery... they're about half a foot away from the ceiling already.

The desk is a bit cluttered. Not too messy, though. But not orderly, either. I wonder what her room at home looks like?

It's her colorful little corner at the office. Rosanna approves of it, as I heard her say that. I don't know if Virginia would, if she sees it. - Juli

ink polaroids are short things. limitations dictate the writer to be bloody descriptive. they push the writer to show us a scene through words, after all, polaroids are supposed to be visual so the words, so concise, should conjure up images in your head, as if you're looking at a real photograph. the name 'INK POLAROIDS' was invented by Stuart David of the band Looper. we enjoyed the concept so much that we're posting our own polaroids here. check out belle and sebastian's ink polaroids.
